# Performance validation checklist

Use a connected YouTube Music account and test both a cold cache and a warm cache.

- Open Home and confirm the first interaction remains responsive while artwork finishes.
- Confirm delayed covers appear without replacing or flashing the entire Home tree.
- Resize the window through compact, normal desktop and wide layouts; Home sections should reflow around 2, 3, 4, 6, 8 or 10 cards per row.
- Open a Home track and verify its section queue order is preserved.
- Open Queue 2.0 with short and long queues, then drag entries through several positions while playback continues.
- Navigate repeatedly between Home, albums, artists, playlists and Queue 2.0 while monitoring CPU and memory.
- Revisit Home and Queue 2.0 to confirm shared artwork textures are reused.
